Our Goals

Our Goals are simple. To save as many animals as we can. Yes thats correct, all animals. Although we started out with only caring for house pets, we now attempt to save all animals, however we do not market it. To the public we mostly focus on the pets side of our operation because it is what most people care about. However, for those who have concerns that we only care for dogs and cats, rest assured that we work hard to take care of all creatures.

We strive for animal education. Before we allow one of our furry friends to be taken to their new families, we require that they take a quick 15-30 min with a caretaker of the animal where they will go over how to feed the animal, what they should and shouldn't eat, and to instruct them if the animal requires any special care. If they discover that taking care of that specific pet is to difficult for them, that same caretaker will personally halp them to find the right pet for them. we tell them that if they would like we could keep the certain pet they chose on hold for a maximum of 3 days to give them an opportunity to think about it. To make a long story short, we strive to give every animal the best life they can and if we can, to give them to a family that will love and care for them as well.
