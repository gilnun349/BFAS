Past Events

We have had many fun events in the past.

Down below we have just a few

We rented local pools in some towns and invited locals to bring there pets to the pool for a swim! We of course helped with all the cleaning required and had many fun games for pets big and small. We even had cats in on the fun. Our volunteers handed out some free toys for the pets at the end of the day. We plan to have a beach day soon.
We had many fun games and toys for all pets big and small. We invited everyone to visit our website to see if a park near them would be holding this event. Throughout the nation we had over 5000 visitors bring there pets. We took the opportunity to also give help on how to properly take care of pets and even sick pets. There were alot of families who were passing by that asked us how they could adopt pets. We gave away some free treats to everyones pets. All the pets loved playing with all the other pets and their owners. Look out for a Park Day in 2019 this summer.
We asked local university as well as big name universities like USC UCLA Cal Poly Pomona Cal State Fullerton etc... if we could use some of there rooms during the summer to hold some free activity/seminars where if anyone had questions about there pets whether it be medical or just in general we answered it for them. There were many people who came in with there pets who had infections and while many were very simple fixes there were some that we recommened to take to a veterinarian. We told them they could bring them to our pet hospital and for what price, if it was to expensive for them we recommended them other veterinarians where they may be able to get a cheaper price.
